# Random Tactical Timer — 30-Day Action Plan (Brand Track B)

## Positioning Lock (Non-Negotiable)
- Core message: Train for chaos. Not comfort.
- Market narrow, allow broad.
- Primary audience: boxing/BJJ/MMA/combatives coaches and trainees.
- Secondary audience (description only): HIIT/CrossFit/functional fitness.

## Weekly Output (4 Weeks)
- 2 short videos/week (8 total)
- 1 technical authority post/week (4 total)
- 5 gym outreach DMs/week (20 total)
- 5 direct review asks/week to active users (20 total)

## Content Sprint

### Video format A (demo)
- Scene: pads, bag, scramble, wall-work, shadowboxing.
- Overlay line 1: Most people train in predictable rounds.
- Overlay line 2: Real fights don't.
- End frame: Random Tactical Timer (App Store).

### Video format B (education)
- Prompt: "If you always know when the round ends, you train anticipation, not reaction."
- Close: "Train for chaos. Not comfort."

## 4-Week Calendar

### Week 1
- Publish 2 videos (one demo, one education).
- Publish post: Why predictable timers reduce reaction quality.
- DM 5 local gyms (feedback ask, not promotion ask).

### Week 2
- Publish 2 videos (boxing + BJJ contexts).
- Publish post: Building randomized interval behavior in iOS.
- DM 5 gyms + follow-up Week 1 contacts.

### Week 3
- Publish 2 videos (combatives + conditioning contexts).
- Publish post: Stress inoculation and pattern interrupts in training.
- Ask top 5 active users for App Store reviews.

### Week 4
- Publish 2 videos (best-performing format + remix).
- Publish post: What reaction-randomization changed in real sessions.
- Review conversion funnel and lock next 30-day plan.

## Review Velocity Playbook
- Ask after user has completed at least 3 sessions.
- Script:
  - "Has this changed your training quality?"
  - If yes: "A short App Store review helps a lot."
- Goal: +10 to +20 niche reviews in 30 days.

## Gym Outreach Script (DM)
"Hey coach — I built a free random reaction timer for fight drills (boxing/BJJ/combatives). Not asking for promo; just want real feedback from class use. If you want to test it in one session, I'd love your notes."

## KPI Targets (30 days)
- 8 videos published
- 4 authority posts published
- 20 coach outreach attempts
- 10+ meaningful user feedback replies
- 10+ new niche App Store reviews

## Kill Criteria (avoid distraction)
- No paid ads in first 30 days.
- No subscription launch in first 30 days.
- No broad "for everyone" messaging.
