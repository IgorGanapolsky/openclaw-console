# Privacy Policy for OpenClaw Work Console

**Last updated:** March 2, 2026

## Overview

OpenClaw Work Console ("the App") is a mobile client that connects to your self-hosted OpenClaw gateway. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect:
- Personal information
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports (unless you opt in)
- Location data
- Device identifiers

## Data Storage

All data handled by the App stays between your device and your self-hosted OpenClaw gateway:
- **Gateway tokens** are stored in platform-secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) on your device only.
- **Agent data, tasks, incidents, and approvals** are fetched from and stored on your gateway server, which you own and operate.
- No data is sent to our servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Network Communication

The App communicates exclusively with OpenClaw gateway endpoints that you configure. We recommend using TLS (HTTPS/WSS) and VPN for all connections.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
